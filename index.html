Welcome!

Welcome to Case Clicker 2.

The successor to my old game (now called) Case Clicker Classic!

If you know my old game, this should be very similar. Start your game by going to your inventory and try opening the case there.

To get more cases, go to the Earn tab and click to earn money. With the money you can buy new cases from the shop.

To gamble your skins, see what's inside the Games tab.
